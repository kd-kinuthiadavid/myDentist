Services



Teeth Replacement

Dental implants are screw-like replacements, for the roots of teeth that are missing or have been removed. Dental implants are in the jawbone permanently, are very stable and very comfortable and you will be able to eat all your favourite food with it. You do not have to replace every single missing tooth with an implant. One implant can carry more than one tooth.

Teeth Whitening

Your teeth consist out of small ” tubes”. With time these “tubes” gets clogged up and basically we clean those “tubes” during the procedure and whiten them in the same process.

Quality Braces

Your teeth consist out of small ” tubes”. With time these “tubes” gets wide and basically we insert braces to narrow the widen gap in the Teeth.
